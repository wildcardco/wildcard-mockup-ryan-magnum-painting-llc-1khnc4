About Magnum Painting

A Local Painter Who Shows Up and Sweats the Details

Magnum Painting LLC is based right in St. John, Indiana, and takes on painting projects for homeowners and small businesses throughout the surrounding communities. As an owner-operated company, every job gets close attention — from surface prep and taping to the final coat.
